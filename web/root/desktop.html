Welcome to DigyttPlace!


Tap to place a pixel.


Pinch or use + and - buttons to zoom.


Long press to copy a colour from an existing pixel.


To change colour, choose any colour from the colour picker in the bottom left corner.


To open this again at any time, use the "Info" button in the top left corner of the screen.
